Hello Everyone! Thank you for checking out this webpage. I am an assistant professor of mathematics at California State University, Sacramento. My fields of interests are primarily partial differential equations, numerical analysis, and functional analysis. In particular, I am interested in the theoretical and numerical study of differential equations on manifolds, properties of function spaces on manifolds, and their applications in mathematical physics. I also have a passion for teaching math.
